Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Idle Realm ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information within the Pi Network ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate via the Pi Network SDK, we may collect:

  • Your Pi Network username.
  • Your Pi Network Wallet Address (public key).
  • Your unique User Identifier (UID) provided by Pi Network.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Identify you within the game environment.
  • Process User-to-App and App-to-User payments.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our test environment.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application operates through the Pi Network SDK and is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. These services may collect metadata as part of their standard operations.

4. Data Retention

As this is a test environment, your data may be cleared periodically. We do not store your private keys or passphrase; these are never accessible to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us through the official Pi Network community channels.
